Good Trouble Manhattan Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz. Good Trouble Bourbon
  • 1 oz. Sweet Vermouth
  • 2-3 Dashes Angostura Bitters
  • Luxardo Original Maraschino Cherry (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing glass filled with ice, combine Good Trouble Bourbon, sweet vermouth, and Angostura bitters.
  2. Stir well to chill the mixture.
  3. Strain into a chilled martini or rocks glass.
  4. Garnish with an Original Maraschino Cherry.
