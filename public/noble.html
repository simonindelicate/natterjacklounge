Noble Embrace Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 ½ oz. Good Trouble Bourbon
  • ½ oz. Tempus Fugit Crème de Cocoa
  • 2 Dashes of Aztec Chocolate Bitters
  • 2 Dashes of Orange Bitters
  • Orange Twist over Large Cube (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Add all ingredients to mixing glass, add ice and stir thoroughly.
  2. Single strain over one large cube in a double-wide old-fashioned glass.
  3. Garnish with an orange twist and serve.

Backstory: Revisiting the classic Old Fashioned with a universally loved twist, this cocktail pairs bourbon with chocolate and orange bitters. It's a celebration of flavors that brings people together, showcasing the timeless appeal of chocolate and orange.
