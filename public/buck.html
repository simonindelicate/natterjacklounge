Kentucky Buck Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 fresh Strawberry (hulled, small & ripe)
  • 2 fluid ounces Bourbon Whiskey
  • 3/4 fluid ounce Lemon Juice (freshly squeezed)
  • 1/4 fluid ounce Sugar Syrup 'rich' (2 sugar to 1 water)
  • 2 dashes Angostura Aromatic Bitters
  • Ginger Beer to top up

Instructions:

  1. Muddle strawberry in base of shaker.
  2. Add next 4 ingredients, shake with ice and fine strain into ice-filled glass.
  3. Top with ginger beer.
  4. Garnish with lemon slice & strawberry slice.
