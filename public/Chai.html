Chai-Infused Good Trouble Martini Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz Chai-infused Good Trouble Bourbon (infuse bourbon with chai tea bags for 24 hours)
  • 1/2 oz Vanilla Liqueur
  • 1/4 oz Cinnamon Syrup
  • Garnish: Orange Twist
  • Glassware: Martini Glass

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing glass filled with ice, combine Chai-infused Good Trouble Bourbon, vanilla liqueur, and cinnamon syrup.
  2. Stir well to chill the mixture.
  3. Strain into a chilled martini glass.
  4. Express the oil from an orange twist over the cocktail and use it as a garnish.
