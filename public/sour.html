Whiskey Sour Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 fluid ounces Whiskey
  • 2 fluid ounces Fresh Lemon Juice
  • 1 fluid ounce Simple Syrup
  • 1 cup Ice Cubes (or as needed)
  • 3 Maraschino Cherries (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. In a shaker, combine whiskey, lemon juice, and simple syrup.
  2. Add ice and shake until well chilled.
  3. Strain into a glass filled with ice.
  4. Garnish with cherries.
