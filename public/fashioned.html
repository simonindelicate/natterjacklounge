Old Fashioned Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 50ml Bourbon Whisky
  • 10ml Demerara Sugar Syrup or Brown Sugar Cube
  • 2 Dashes Of Angostura Bitters
  • Orange Zest

Instructions:

  1. Cut a few discs of orange zest and add to the rocks glass. Muddle the orange gently to start releasing the oil.
  2. Add sugar cube if using and carefully add two dashes to the cube to help break it down. Otherwise add the sugar syrup.
  3. Pour half the whiskey into the glass and three cubes of ice and stir for thirty seconds.
  4. Repeat with the remainder of whiskey and more ice and stir again.
  5. Squeeze a further orange zest over the cocktail and serve.
