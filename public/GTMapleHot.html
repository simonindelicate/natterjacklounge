Good Trouble Maple Hot Toddy Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz. Good Trouble Bourbon
  • 1 oz Pure Maple Syrup
  • 1/2 oz Fresh Lemon Juice
  • Hot Water
  • Garnish: Cinnamon Stick and Lemon Wheel
  • Glassware: Heat Resistant Glass

Instructions:

  1. In a heat-resistant glass, combine Good Trouble Bourbon, pure maple syrup, and fresh lemon juice.
  2. Fill the glass with hot water and stir.
  3. Garnish with a cinnamon stick and a lemon wheel.
