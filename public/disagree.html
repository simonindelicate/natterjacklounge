Disagree to Agree Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 oz. Good Trouble Bourbon
  • ½ oz. VSOP Cognac
  • 1 oz. Fresh Squeezed Lime Juice
  • 1 ½ oz. Beet Sugar Simple Syrup (or Cane Sugar Syrup)
  • 5 Large Leaves of Freshly Muddled Mint
  • Lime Wedge and Large Mint Sprig (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Add lime, sugar syrup, and mint to mixing tin, muddle thoroughly.
  2. Add Cognac, Good Trouble Bourbon, and large ice, shake vigorously.
  3. Double strain over fresh ice into a double-wide old fashioned glass.
  4. Garnish with lime wedge and large mint sprig, then serve.

Backstory: Emphasizing commonality over differences, this cocktail is a modern twist on the Mint Julep, blending bourbon and cognac. It's a testament to finding unity in diversity, symbolizing how different elements can create harmonious 'Good Trouble.'
