Good Trouble Fig and Rosemary Smash Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz. Good Trouble Bourbon
  • 2 Fresh Figs, Muddled
  • 1/2 oz Honey Syrup (1:1 ratio of honey and water)
  • Garnish: Fresh Rosemary Sprig
  • Glassware: Rocks Glass

Instructions:

  1. In a shaker, muddle fresh figs.
  2. Add Good Trouble Bourbon and honey syrup.
  3. Shake well and strain into a rocks glass filled with ice.
  4. Garnish with a fresh rosemary sprig.
