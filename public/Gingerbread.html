Good Trouble Gingerbread Flip Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz Good Trouble Bourbon
  • 1/2 oz Gingerbread Syrup (molasses, brown sugar, ginger, and water)
  • 1 Whole Egg
  • Garnish: Grated Nutmeg
  • Glassware: Chilled Coupe Glass

Instructions:

  1. In a shaker, combine Good Trouble Bourbon, gingerbread syrup, and a whole egg.
  2. Dry shake (shake without ice) for about 10 seconds.
  3. Add ice to the shaker and shake again vigorously.
  4. Strain into a chilled coupe glass.
  5. Garnish with grated nutmeg.
