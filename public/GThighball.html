Good Trouble Bourbon and Ginger Ale Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz. Good Trouble Bourbon
  • Ginger Ale
  • Lemon Wedge (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Fill a highball glass with ice.
  2. Add Good Trouble Bourbon to the glass.
  3. Top with ginger ale.
  4. Stir gently to mix.
  5. Garnish with a lemon wedge.
