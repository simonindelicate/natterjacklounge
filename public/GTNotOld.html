Good Trouble Not Old-Fashioned Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz. Good Trouble Bourbon
  • 1 Sugar Cube
  • 2-3 Dashes Angostura Bitters
  • Orange Twist (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Muddle the sugar cube and a splash of water in a glass.
  2. Add Good Trouble Bourbon and bitters.
  3. Fill the glass with ice and stir well.
  4. Garnish with an orange twist.
