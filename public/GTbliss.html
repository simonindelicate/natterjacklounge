Good Trouble Bourbon Punch Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 Cups Good Trouble Bourbon
  • 1/2 Cup Peach Schnapps
  • 1 Cup Orange Juice
  • 1/2 Cup Cranberry Juice
  • 1/4 Cup Simple Syrup
  • Sliced Peaches and Orange Wheels (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. In a punch bowl, combine Good Trouble Bourbon, peach schnapps, orange juice, cranberry juice, and simple syrup.
  2. Stir well to mix.
  3. Add ice to the punch bowl.
  4. Garnish with sliced peaches and orange wheels.
  5. Serve in individual glasses over ice.
