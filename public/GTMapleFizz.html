Good Trouble Smoked Maple Fizz Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz. Good Trouble Bourbon
  • 1/2 oz Pure Maple Syrup
  • 1/2 oz Fresh Lemon Juice
  • 1 Dash of Smoked Bitters
  • Club Soda
  • Smoked Sea Salt (for rimming)
  • Garnish: Lemon Peel
  • Glassware: Highball Glass

Instructions:

  1. Rim a highball glass with smoked sea salt.
  2. In a shaker filled with ice, combine Good Trouble Bourbon, maple syrup, fresh lemon juice, and smoked bitters.
  3. Shake well and strain into the prepared highball glass.
  4. Top with club soda.
