Troublemaker Sour Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz. Good Trouble Bourbon
  • 3/4 oz. Fresh Lemon Juice
  • 1/2 oz. Simple Syrup
  • 1 Egg White
  • Angostura Bitters (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Combine Good Trouble Bourbon, lemon juice, simple syrup, and egg white in a shaker without ice.
  2. Dry shake for about 10 seconds to emulsify the egg white.
  3. Add ice to the shaker and shake vigorously for another 10-15 seconds.
  4. Strain into a chilled glass.
  5. Garnish with a few drops of Angostura bitters on the foam.
