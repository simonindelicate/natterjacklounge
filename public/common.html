Common Ground Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 oz. Good Trouble Bourbon
  • 1 oz. Aperol
  • ½ oz. Fresh Lemon Juice
  • 2 ½ oz. Moscato D’asti (Sparkling Wine)
  • Freshly Muddled Tarragon
  • Tarragon Sprig (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Fill glassware with large ice, add all ingredients except Moscato to mixing tin and muddle tarragon.
  2. Double strain contents over ice in glass, slowly pour Moscato D’asti taking care not to foam over.
  3. Use bar spoon to mix ingredients together, garnish with tarragon sprig, and serve.

Backstory: A fusion of popular choices among women, this cocktail combines the Aperol Spritz and bourbon, accented with Moscato D’asti and tarragon. It's a delightful blend that can bring people together, finding unexpected common ground.
