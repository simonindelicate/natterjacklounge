Mint Julep Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 5 Fresh Mint Leaves (roughly torn)
  • 1 ounce Simple Syrup (adjust per your tastes)
  • Ice (crushed)
  • splash Cold Water
  • 2 ounces Bourbon
  • Fresh Mint sprig (for garnish - optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a julep cup or glass, muddle the mint leaves and simple syrup until the mint is fragrant.
  2. Add the bourbon and fill the cup with crushed ice. Stir until the cup is frosted on the outside.
  3. Add more crushed ice to form an ice dome and garnish with a mint sprig.
