Walk a Mile in My Stilettos Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ oz. Good Trouble Bourbon
  • ½ oz. Amaretto
  • 1 oz. Fresh Lime Juice
  • 1 oz. Cane Sugar Water
  • 1 oz. Cranberry Juice
  • 2 Dashes of Cardamon Bitters
  • Lime Wedge (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Add all ingredients to a mixing tin.
  2. Add ice and shake vigorously.
  3. Double strain over fresh ice into a highball or chimney glass.
  4. Garnish with a lime wedge and serve.

Backstory: Ginger Rodgers famously said that she had to do everything Fred Astaire did, only backwards and in high heels! When women of good conscience get together, they can solve the problems of the world. People don’t need to always agree to listen and thoughtfully engage in a productive conversation. This modern take on the classic STILETTO cocktail brings all people together and allows them to thoughtfully see what it’s like to walk a mile in someone else’s stilettos!
