Spiced Cranberry Good Trouble Punch Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz. Good Trouble Bourbon
  • 1 oz Spiced Cranberry Syrup (cranberries, sugar, water, cinnamon, and cloves)
  • 1/2 oz Orange Liqueur
  • 1/2 oz Fresh Lime Juice
  • Club Soda
  • Garnish: Fresh Cranberries and Rosemary Sprig

Instructions:

  1. In a shaker with ice, combine Good Trouble Bourbon, spiced cranberry syrup, orange liqueur, and fresh lime juice.
  2. Shake well and strain into a glass filled with ice.
  3. Top with club soda.
  4. Garnish with fresh cranberries and a rosemary sprig.
